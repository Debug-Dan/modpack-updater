--- FAQ ---

Q: Why is the download a .bat file?
A: Unlike a traditional application, this launcher runs entirely in the command line. A .bat file is just a script that runs in Command Prompt; this allows the launcher to be extremely lightweight and small in size.

Q: I'm having an issue with something in the launcher. Can you help?
A: Yes! The launcher comes with a built-in troubleshooting feature. Just hit option 4 on the main menu.
